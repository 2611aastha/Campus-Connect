About Us

About Campus Connect

Campus Connect is your all-in-one digital companion for navigating campus life at Thapar University. We've created this platform to bridge the gap between scattered WhatsApp groups, confusing campus layouts, and missed opportunities.

What We Offer:

  • Interactive campus map with all locations
  • Centralized events hub for all college activities
  • Digital notice board for important announcements
  • Social space for lost & found, ride-sharing, and community polls
  • Clean, user-friendly interface designed by students, for students

Our mission is to make campus navigation effortless and keep you connected with everything happening around you.

About Thapar University

Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology (TIET), commonly known as Thapar University, is a premier private engineering institution located in Patiala, Punjab, India. Established in 1956, it has a rich legacy of academic excellence and innovation.

Key Highlights:

  • Ranked among India's top engineering institutions
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and modern facilities
  • Vibrant campus life with 50+ clubs and societies
  • Strong industry connections and placement opportunities
  • Sprawling green campus with world-class amenities
  • Focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship

Thapar University continues to shape future engineers, innovators, and leaders who make a difference in the world.
